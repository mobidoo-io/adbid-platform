Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I exceed my ad spend limit?

We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your limit. You can upgrade your plan anytime, and we'll prorate the difference.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, all plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for Enterprise accounts.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for any plan. Enterprise customers may have one-time onboarding fees for custom implementations.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. You'll have access until the end of your billing period.
