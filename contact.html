Frequently asked questions

How quickly can I start working with Adbid? +

Registration and basic setup takes about 10 minutes. After connecting your ad accounts, you can immediately start analyzing data. For complete automation setup, we recommend conducting an onboarding session with our specialist.

Which advertising platforms does Adbid support? +

Currently, we support Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, Google Ads, TikTok Ads, and Snapchat Ads. Integrations with other platforms are added regularly based on customer requests.

Do I need to provide access to my advertising accounts? +

Adbid connects to your accounts through official platform APIs with minimal access rights. We only receive read-only data for analytics, without the ability to change settings without your explicit permission.

Is there a trial period? +

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial period with full functionality. No credit card is required to start the trial period.

Can Adbid be integrated with our internal systems? +

Yes, we provide REST API and webhooks for integration with CRMs, analytics systems, and other tools. Ready-made integrations with popular platforms are also available.
